About Me

As a current master's student in User Experience Design at the University of the Arts London and an undergraduate in Experimental Media Arts, I bring a seamless blend of the digital and physical realms to my design approach. With expertise in design research, my skill set spans proficiency in emerging technologies and a diverse range of design methodologies. Adept at insightful analysis in my design research, I'm driven by a deep interest in the convergence of technology and human interactions, motivating me to push the boundaries of design excellence.
